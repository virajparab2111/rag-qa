Cancellation Policy

Order cancellations are subject to the following terms:

1. Cancellation Before Shipping
- Orders may be canceled at no cost if the cancellation request is made before the order has been shipped.
- Customers must submit the cancellation request through their account dashboard or by contacting customer support.

2. Cancellation After Shipping
- Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be canceled.
- Customers may instead initiate a return after delivery, subject to the Refund Policy.

3. Subscription Cancellations
- Subscription-based services may be canceled at any time.
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
- No partial refunds are provided for unused subscription periods.

4. Company-Initiated Cancellations
- The company reserves the right to cancel orders due to stock unavailability, pricing errors, or suspected fraudulent activity.
- In such cases, customers will receive a full refund.
