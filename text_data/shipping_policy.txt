Shipping Policy

We aim to process and ship all orders in a timely manner according to the following guidelines:

1. Order Processing
- Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation.
- Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next business day.

2. Shipping Methods
- Standard shipping is available for all domestic orders.
- Express shipping options may be available at an additional cost.

3. Delivery Timeframes
- Standard shipping typically takes 5–7 business days.
- Express shipping typically takes 2–3 business days.
- Delivery times may vary depending on location and courier service.

4. International Shipping
- International shipping is available to selected countries.
- Customs duties, taxes, and import fees are the responsibility of the customer.

5. Delayed or Lost Shipments
- If a shipment is delayed beyond the estimated delivery window, customers should contact customer support.
- The company is not responsible for delays caused by customs, natural disasters, or courier issues.

6. Incorrect Shipping Information
- Customers are responsible for providing accurate shipping information.
- The company is not liable for orders shipped to incorrectly provided addresses.
